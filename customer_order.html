Orders, Customers, OrderItems, Payments

This form collects data for Customers, Orders, OrderItems, and Payments. In Phase 4, it will submit to PHP/SQL.

Customer

Matches relation: Customers(CustomerID, Name, Email, Phone, Address)
Existing ID, or leave blank for new customers (DB will generate later).

Order

Matches relation: Orders(OrderID, CustomerID, EmployeeID, OrderDate, Status, Channel, TotalAmount)
Can be auto-generated later; included here to match the schema.

Order Items

Matches relation: OrderItems(OrderID, LineNo, ItemID, Quantity)

For simplicity, this interface shows three order lines. Line numbers correspond to LineNo.

Payment

Matches relation: Payments(OrderID, PaymentNo, Method, Amount, Status)
